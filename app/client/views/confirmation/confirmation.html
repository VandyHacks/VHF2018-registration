Travel

Our reimbursement policy is as follows:

  • Bus: We will be offering buses to Illinois Institute of Technology, Purdue, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Georgia Institute of Technology, University of Florida, and Washington University in St. Louis. If you can find your own way to any of the stops, you can board the bus. We will require reservation of bus seats ahead of time (this will be released prior to the event).
  • Car: We will generally reimburse all gas costs for cars containing 3 or more hackers. We may offer partial reimbursement to cars containing fewer hackers.
  • Flight & Greyhounds: Reimbursement is generally $75-$100. However, we may consider on a case by case basis small reimbursements (~$50) comparable to the reimbursement hackers who drive would receive. Contact us for more information.

If you have another way to get to VandyHacks (or just have questions), email us at travel@vandyhacks.org.
